The Opportunity
Strong Reputation.
No Digital Front Door.
Erkan Auto Care has the reputation to compete with any shop in Livermore. The gap is entirely digital. Here's what's holding new customers back today.

No website

Drivers can't find hours, services, or a way to book online. Everything today runs through phone calls and word-of-mouth.

No booking system

Every appointment requires a phone call during business hours. There's no way to request a time online, day or night.

Reviews aren't centralized

Strong word-of-mouth doesn't show up anywhere a new customer would actually look before choosing a shop.

No search visibility

Erkan Auto Care won't show up for "brake repair Livermore" or similar searches — today, or after launch without ongoing work.

None of this is a knock on the shop — it's an opportunity. The work is already good. It just isn't visible yet.
